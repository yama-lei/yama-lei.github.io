Abstract  摘要

We propose the task of free-form and open-ended Visual Question Answering (VQA). Given an image and a natural language question about the image, the task is to provide an accurate natural language answer. Mirroring real-world scenarios, such as helping the visually impaired, both the questions and answers are open-ended. Visual questions selectively target different areas of an image, including background details and underlying context. As a result, a system that succeeds at VQA typically needs a more detailed understanding of the image and complex reasoning than a system producing generic image captions. Moreover, VQA is amenable to automatic evaluation, since many open-ended answers contain only a few words or a closed set of answers that can be provided in a multiple-choice format. We provide a dataset containing ∼similar-to\sim0.25M images, ∼similar-to\sim0.76M questions, and ∼similar-to\sim10M answers (www.visualqa.org), and discuss the information it provides. Numerous baselines and methods for VQA are provided and compared with human performance. Our VQA demo is available on CloudCV (http://cloudcv.org/vqa).
我们提出了自由形式和开放式视觉问答（VQA）的任务。给定一张图像和一个关于该图像的自然语言问题，任务是提供一个准确的自然语言答案。为了反映现实世界的场景，例如帮助视障人士，问题和答案都是开放式的。视觉问题有选择地针对图像的不同区域，包括背景细节和潜在上下文。因此，一个在 VQA 任务中成功的系统通常需要比生成通用图像描述的系统更详细地理解图像并进行复杂的推理。此外，VQA 适合自动评估，因为许多开放式答案只包含几个单词或可以在多项选择格式中提供的封闭答案集。我们提供了一个包含 ∼similar-to\sim 25 万张图像、 ∼similar-to\sim 76 万个问题和 ∼similar-to\sim 1000 万个答案的数据集（www.visualqa.org），并讨论了它提供的信息。提供了许多 VQA 的基线和方法，并与人类表现进行了比较。我们的 VQA 演示可在 CloudCV 上找到（http://cloudcv.org/vqa）。

1 Introduction  1 引言

We are witnessing a renewed excitement in multi-discipline Artificial Intelligence (AI) research problems. In particular, research in image and video captioning that combines Computer Vision (CV), Natural Language Processing (NLP), and Knowledge Representation & Reasoning (KR) has dramatically increased in the past year [16, 9, 12, 38, 26, 24, 53]. Part of this excitement stems from a belief that multi-discipline tasks like image captioning are a step towards solving AI. However, the current state of the art demonstrates that a coarse scene-level understanding of an image paired with word n𝑛n-gram statistics suffices to generate reasonable image captions, which suggests image captioning may not be as “AI-complete” as desired.
我们正见证着多学科人工智能（AI）研究问题重新焕发的热情。特别是，结合计算机视觉（CV）、自然语言处理（NLP）和知识表示与推理（KR）的图像和视频字幕研究在过去一年中显著增加 [16, 9, 12, 38, 26, 24, 53]。这种热情部分源于一种信念，即像图像字幕这样的多学科任务是迈向解决 AI 的一步。然而，当前的最新技术表明，对图像的粗略场景级理解加上单词 nn -gram 统计足以生成合理的图像字幕，这表明图像字幕可能并不像人们所期望的那样“AI 完备”。

What makes for a compelling “AI-complete” task? We believe that in order to spawn the next generation of AI algorithms, an ideal task should (i) require multi-modal knowledge beyond a single sub-domain (such as CV) and (ii) have a well-defined quantitative evaluation metric to track progress. For some tasks, such as image captioning, automatic evaluation is still a difficult and open research problem [51, 13, 22].
什么构成了一个引人注目的“AI 完备”任务？我们认为，为了催生下一代 AI 算法，理想的任务应该（i）需要超越单一子领域（如 CV）的多模态知识，并且（ii）具有明确的定量评估指标以跟踪进展。对于某些任务，如图像字幕，自动评估仍然是一个困难且开放的研究问题 [51, 13, 22]。

In this paper, we introduce the task of free-form and open-ended Visual Question Answering (VQA). A VQA system takes as input an image and a free-form, open-ended, natural-language question about the image and produces a natural-language answer as the output. This goal-driven task is applicable to scenarios encountered when visually-impaired users [3] or intelligence analysts actively elicit visual information. Example questions are shown in Fig. 1.
在本文中，我们介绍了自由形式和开放式视觉问答（VQA）任务。VQA 系统接收一张图像和一个关于该图像的自由形式、开放式自然语言问题作为输入，并生成一个自然语言答案作为输出。这一目标驱动的任务适用于视障用户[3]或情报分析师主动获取视觉信息时遇到的场景。示例问题如图 1 所示。

Open-ended questions require a potentially vast set of AI capabilities to answer – fine-grained recognition (e.g., “What kind of cheese is on the pizza?”), object detection (e.g., “How many bikes are there?”), activity recognition (e.g., “Is this man crying?”), knowledge base reasoning (e.g., “Is this a vegetarian pizza?”), and commonsense reasoning (e.g., “Does this person have 20/20 vision?”, “Is this person expecting company?”). VQA [19, 36, 50, 3] is also amenable to automatic quantitative evaluation, making it possible to effectively track progress on this task. While the answer to many questions is simply “yes” or “no”, the process for determining a correct answer is typically far from trivial (e.g. in Fig. 1, “Does this person have 20/20 vision?”). Moreover, since questions about images often tend to seek specific information, simple one-to-three word answers are sufficient for many questions. In such scenarios, we can easily evaluate a proposed algorithm by the number of questions it answers correctly. In this paper, we present both an open-ended answering task and a multiple-choice task [45, 33]. Unlike the open-ended task that requires a free-form response, the multiple-choice task only requires an algorithm to pick from a predefined list of possible answers.
开放式问题需要一系列潜在的广泛 AI 能力来回答——细粒度识别（例如，“披萨上是什么类型的奶酪？”）、物体检测（例如，“有多少辆自行车？”）、活动识别（例如，“这个人在哭吗？”）、知识库推理（例如，“这是素食披萨吗？”）以及常识推理（例如，“这个人的视力是 20/20 吗？”，“这个人是否在等人？”）。VQA [19, 36, 50, 3] 也适合自动定量评估，使得有效跟踪该任务的进展成为可能。虽然许多问题的答案只是“是”或“否”，但确定正确答案的过程通常远非简单（例如，在图 1 中，“这个人的视力是 20/20 吗？”）。此外，由于关于图像的问题通常倾向于寻求特定信息，简单的一到三个单词的答案对于许多问题来说已经足够。在这种情况下，我们可以通过算法正确回答的问题数量轻松评估所提出的算法。在本文中，我们提出了一个开放式回答任务和一个多项选择任务 [45, 33]。 与需要自由形式回答的开放式任务不同，选择题任务仅要求算法从预定义的可能答案列表中进行选择。

We present a large dataset that contains 204,721 images from the MS COCO dataset [32] and a newly created abstract scene dataset [57, 2] that contains 50,000 scenes. The MS COCO dataset has images depicting diverse and complex scenes that are effective at eliciting compelling and diverse questions. We collected a new dataset of “realistic” abstract scenes to enable research focused only on the high-level reasoning required for VQA by removing the need to parse real images. Three questions were collected for each image or scene. Each question was answered by ten subjects along with their confidence. The dataset contains over 760K questions with around 10M answers.
我们提出了一个大型数据集，其中包含来自 MS COCO 数据集[32]的 204,721 张图像以及一个新创建的抽象场景数据集[57, 2]，该数据集包含 50,000 个场景。MS COCO 数据集中的图像描绘了多样且复杂的场景，这些场景能够有效地引发引人入胜且多样化的问题。我们收集了一个新的“逼真”抽象场景数据集，通过消除解析真实图像的需求，使研究仅专注于 VQA 所需的高级推理。每张图像或场景收集了三个问题。每个问题由十名受试者回答，并附上他们的信心评分。该数据集包含超过 760K 个问题，约有 10M 个答案。

While the use of open-ended questions offers many benefits, it is still useful to understand the types of questions that are being asked and which types various algorithms may be good at answering. To this end, we analyze the types of questions asked and the types of answers provided. Through several visualizations, we demonstrate the astonishing diversity of the questions asked. We also explore how the information content of questions and their answers differs from image captions. For baselines, we offer several approaches that use a combination of both text and state-of-the-art visual features [29]. As part of the VQA initiative, we will organize an annual challenge and associated workshop to discuss state-of-the-art methods and best practices.
虽然开放式问题的使用带来了许多好处，但了解所提问题的类型以及各种算法可能擅长回答的类型仍然很有用。为此，我们分析了所提问题的类型和提供的答案类型。通过几种可视化方式，我们展示了所提问题的惊人多样性。我们还探讨了问题及其答案的信息内容与图像描述之间的差异。作为基线，我们提供了几种结合文本和最先进视觉特征的方法[29]。作为 VQA 计划的一部分，我们将组织年度挑战赛和相关研讨会，讨论最先进的方法和最佳实践。

VQA poses a rich set of challenges, many of which have been viewed as the holy grail of automatic image understanding and AI in general. However, it includes as building blocks several components that the CV, NLP, and KR [5, 8, 31, 35, 4] communities have made significant progress on during the past few decades. VQA provides an attractive balance between pushing the state of the art, while being accessible enough for the communities to start making progress on the task.
VQA 提出了一系列丰富的挑战，其中许多被视为自动图像理解和 AI 的圣杯。然而，它包含了 CV、NLP 和 KR [5, 8, 31, 35, 4] 社区在过去几十年中取得显著进展的几个组件。VQA 在推动技术前沿的同时，也为社区提供了足够容易上手的任务，使其能够开始取得进展。

2 Related Work  2 相关工作

VQA Efforts. Several recent papers have begun to study visual question answering [19, 36, 50, 3]. However, unlike our work, these are fairly restricted (sometimes synthetic) settings with small datasets. For instance, [36] only considers questions whose answers come from a predefined closed world of 16 basic colors or 894 object categories. [19] also considers questions generated from templates from a fixed vocabulary of objects, attributes, relationships between objects, etc. In contrast, our proposed task involves open-ended, free-form questions and answers provided by humans. Our goal is to increase the diversity of knowledge and kinds of reasoning needed to provide correct answers. Critical to achieving success on this more difficult and unconstrained task, our VQA dataset is two orders of magnitude larger than [19, 36] (>>250,000 vs. 2,591 and 1,449 images respectively). The proposed VQA task has connections to other related work:  [50] has studied joint parsing of videos and corresponding text to answer queries on two datasets containing 15 video clips each.  [3] uses crowdsourced workers to answer questions about visual content asked by visually-impaired users. In concurrent work, [37] proposed combining an LSTM for the question with a CNN for the image to generate an answer. In their model, the LSTM question representation is conditioned on the CNN image features at each time step, and the final LSTM hidden state is used to sequentially decode the answer phrase. In contrast, the model developed in this paper explores “late fusion” – i.e., the LSTM question representation and the CNN image features are computed independently, fused via an element-wise multiplication, and then passed through fully-connected layers to generate a softmax distribution over output answer classes. [34] generates abstract scenes to capture visual common sense relevant to answering (purely textual) fill-in-the-blank and visual paraphrasing questions. [47] and [52] use visual information to assess the plausibility of common sense assertions. [55] introduced a dataset of 10k images and prompted captions that describe specific aspects of a scene (e.g., individual objects, what will happen next). Concurrent with our work, [18] collected questions & answers in Chinese (later translated to English by humans) for COCO images. [44] automatically generated four types of questions (object, count, color, location) using COCO captions.
VQA 相关工作。最近的几篇论文已经开始研究视觉问答 [19, 36, 50, 3]。然而，与我们的工作不同，这些研究通常是在受限（有时是合成的）环境中使用小数据集进行的。例如，[36] 只考虑那些答案来自预定义的 16 种基本颜色或 894 个对象类别的封闭世界的问题。[19] 也考虑了从固定词汇表中生成的问题，这些词汇表包括对象、属性、对象之间的关系等。相比之下，我们提出的任务涉及由人类提供的开放式、自由形式的问题和答案。我们的目标是增加提供正确答案所需的知识多样性和推理类型。为了在这个更困难且不受约束的任务上取得成功，我们的 VQA 数据集比 [19, 36] 大两个数量级（ >> 250,000 张图像，而 [19, 36] 分别只有 2,591 和 1,449 张图像）。所提出的 VQA 任务与其他相关工作有关：[50] 研究了视频和相应文本的联合解析，以回答包含 15 个视频片段的两个数据集上的查询。[3] 使用众包工作者回答视障用户提出的关于视觉内容的问题。 在并发工作中，[37] 提出将用于问题的 LSTM 与用于图像的 CNN 结合以生成答案。在他们的模型中，LSTM 问题表示在每个时间步上都以 CNN 图像特征为条件，最终的 LSTM 隐藏状态用于顺序解码答案短语。相比之下，本文开发的模型探索了“后期融合”——即 LSTM 问题表示和 CNN 图像特征是独立计算的，通过逐元素乘法融合，然后通过全连接层生成输出答案类别的 softmax 分布。[34] 生成抽象场景以捕捉与回答（纯文本）填空题和视觉释义问题相关的视觉常识。[47] 和 [52] 使用视觉信息来评估常识断言的合理性。[55] 引入了一个包含 10k 张图像的数据集，并提示描述场景特定方面（例如，单个对象、接下来会发生什么）的标题。与我们的工作同时进行的是，[18] 为 COCO 图像收集了中文问答（后来由人工翻译为英文）。 [ 44] 使用 COCO 字幕自动生成了四种类型的问题（对象、数量、颜色、位置）。

Text-based Q&A is a well studied problem in the NLP and text processing communities (recent examples being [15, 14, 54, 45]). Other related textual tasks include sentence completion (e.g., [45] with multiple-choice answers). These approaches provide inspiration for VQA techniques. One key concern in text is the grounding of questions. For instance, [54] synthesized textual descriptions and QA-pairs grounded in a simulation of actors and objects in a fixed set of locations. VQA is naturally grounded in images – requiring the understanding of both text (questions) and vision (images). Our questions are generated by humans, making the need for commonsense knowledge and complex reasoning more essential.
基于文本的问答是 NLP 和文本处理社区中一个被深入研究的问题（最近的例子包括[15, 14, 54, 45]）。其他相关的文本任务包括句子补全（例如，[45]中的多项选择答案）。这些方法为 VQA 技术提供了灵感。文本中的一个关键问题是问题的接地性。例如，[54]合成了基于固定位置中演员和对象模拟的文本描述和问答对。VQA 自然接地于图像——需要理解文本（问题）和视觉（图像）。我们的问题由人类生成，这使得常识知识和复杂推理的需求更加重要。

Describing Visual Content. Related to VQA are the tasks of image tagging [11, 29], image captioning [30, 17, 40, 9, 16, 53, 12, 24, 38, 26] and video captioning [46, 21], where words or sentences are generated to describe visual content. While these tasks require both visual and semantic knowledge, captions can often be non-specific (e.g., observed by [53]). The questions in VQA require detailed specific information about the image for which generic image captions are of little use [3].
描述视觉内容。与视觉问答（VQA）相关的任务包括图像标注 [11, 29]、图像描述生成 [30, 17, 40, 9, 16, 53, 12, 24, 38, 26] 和视频描述生成 [46, 21]，这些任务生成词语或句子来描述视觉内容。虽然这些任务需要视觉和语义知识，但生成的描述往往不够具体（例如，[53] 观察到）。VQA 中的问题需要关于图像的详细具体信息，而通用的图像描述对此帮助不大 [3]。

Other Vision+Language Tasks. Several recent papers have explored tasks at the intersection of vision and language that are easier to evaluate than image captioning, such as coreference resolution [28, 43] or generating referring expressions [25, 42] for a particular object in an image that would allow a human to identify which object is being referred to (e.g., “the one in a red shirt”, “the dog on the left”). While task-driven and concrete, a limited set of visual concepts (e.g., color, location) tend to be captured by referring expressions. As we demonstrate, a richer variety of visual concepts emerge from visual questions and their answers.
其他视觉+语言任务。最近的几篇论文探索了视觉与语言交叉的任务，这些任务比图像描述生成更容易评估，例如共指消解 [28, 43] 或生成指代表达 [25, 42]，用于描述图像中的特定对象，以便人类识别所指的是哪个对象（例如，“穿红衬衫的那个”，“左边的狗”）。虽然这些任务具有驱动性和具体性，但指代表达往往只捕捉到有限的视觉概念（例如，颜色、位置）。正如我们所展示的，视觉问题及其答案中涌现出更丰富的视觉概念。

3 VQA Dataset Collection
3 VQA 数据集收集

We now describe the Visual Question Answering (VQA) dataset. We begin by describing the real images and abstract scenes used to collect the questions. Next, we describe our process of collecting questions and their corresponding answers. Analysis of the questions and answers gathered as well as baselines’ & methods’ results are provided in following sections.
我们现在描述视觉问答（VQA）数据集。首先，我们描述用于收集问题的真实图像和抽象场景。接下来，我们描述收集问题及其对应答案的过程。在后续章节中，提供了对所收集问题和答案的分析以及基线和方法的结果。

Real Images. We use the 123,287 training and validation images and 81,434 test images from the newly-released Microsoft Common Objects in Context (MS COCO) [32] dataset. The MS COCO dataset was gathered to find images containing multiple objects and rich contextual information. Given the visual complexity of these images, they are well-suited for our VQA task. The more diverse our collection of images, the more diverse, comprehensive, and interesting the resultant set of questions and their answers.
真实图像。我们使用了新发布的微软常见物体上下文（MS COCO）[32]数据集中的 123,287 张训练和验证图像以及 81,434 张测试图像。MS COCO 数据集的收集旨在寻找包含多个物体和丰富上下文信息的图像。鉴于这些图像的视觉复杂性，它们非常适合我们的 VQA 任务。我们的图像集合越多样化，生成的问题及其答案就越多样化、全面且有趣。

Abstract Scenes. The VQA task with real images requires the use of complex and often noisy visual recognizers. To attract researchers interested in exploring the high-level reasoning required for VQA, but not the low-level vision tasks, we create a new abstract scenes dataset [2, 57, 58, 59] containing 50K scenes. The dataset contains 20 “paperdoll” human models [2] spanning genders, races, and ages with 8 different expressions. The limbs are adjustable to allow for continuous pose variations. The clipart may be used to depict both indoor and outdoor scenes. The set contains over 100 objects and 31 animals in various poses. The use of this clipart enables the creation of more realistic scenes (see bottom row of Fig. 2) that more closely mirror real images than previous papers [57, 58, 59]. See the appendix for the user interface, additional details, and examples.
抽象场景。使用真实图像的 VQA 任务需要复杂且通常带有噪声的视觉识别器。为了吸引那些对探索 VQA 所需的高级推理感兴趣，但对低级视觉任务不感兴趣的研究人员，我们创建了一个包含 50K 场景的新抽象场景数据集[2, 57, 58, 59]。该数据集包含 20 个“纸娃娃”人类模型[2]，涵盖性别、种族和年龄，具有 8 种不同的表情。肢体可调节，以实现连续的姿势变化。剪贴画可用于描绘室内和室外场景。该集合包含 100 多个对象和 31 种动物，姿势各异。使用这些剪贴画可以创建更逼真的场景（见图 2 的底部行），比之前的论文[57, 58, 59]更接近真实图像。有关用户界面、更多细节和示例，请参阅附录。

Splits. For real images, we follow the same train/val/test split strategy as the MC COCO dataset [32] (including test-dev, test-standard, test-challenge, test-reserve). For the VQA challenge (see section 6), test-dev is used for debugging and validation experiments and allows for unlimited submission to the evaluation server. Test-standard is the ‘default’ test data for the VQA competition. When comparing to the state of the art (e.g., in papers), results should be reported on test-standard. Test-standard is also used to maintain a public leaderboard that is updated upon submission. Test-reserve is used to protect against possible overfitting. If there are substantial differences between a method’s scores on test-standard and test-reserve, this raises a red-flag and prompts further investigation. Results on test-reserve are not publicly revealed. Finally, test-challenge is used to determine the winners of the challenge.
数据集划分。对于真实图像，我们遵循与 MC COCO 数据集[32]相同的训练/验证/测试划分策略（包括测试开发集、测试标准集、测试挑战集和测试保留集）。对于 VQA 挑战赛（见第 6 节），测试开发集用于调试和验证实验，并允许无限制地向评估服务器提交。测试标准集是 VQA 竞赛的‘默认’测试数据。在与最新技术进行比较时（例如在论文中），应在测试标准集上报告结果。测试标准集还用于维护一个公开的排行榜，该排行榜会在提交时更新。测试保留集用于防止可能的过拟合。如果某个方法在测试标准集和测试保留集上的得分存在显著差异，这将引发警示并促使进一步调查。测试保留集的结果不会公开。最后，测试挑战集用于确定挑战赛的获胜者。

Importantly, questions should also require the image to correctly answer and not be answerable using just commonsense information, e.g., in Fig. 1, “What is the mustache made of?”. 5.2 Methods  5.2 方法

For our methods, we develop a 2-channel vision (image) + language (question) model that culminates with a softmax over K𝐾K possible outputs. We choose the top K=1000𝐾1000K=1000 most frequent answers as possible outputs. This set of answers covers 82.67%percent82.6782.67\% of the train+val answers. We describe the different components of our model below:
对于我们的方法，我们开发了一个双通道视觉（图像）+ 语言（问题）模型，最终通过一个 softmax 函数输出 KK 种可能的结果。我们选择前 K=10001000K=1000 个最常见的答案作为可能的输出。这组答案涵盖了训练集和验证集中 82.67%percent82.6782.67\% 的答案。我们在下面描述模型的不同组件：

Image Channel: This channel provides an embedding for the image. We experiment with two embeddings –

  1. 1.

    I: The activations from the last hidden layer of VGGNet [48] are used as 4096-dim image embedding.
    I: 使用 VGGNet [48] 最后一层隐藏层的激活作为 4096 维的图像嵌入。

  2. 2.

    norm I: These are ℓ2subscriptℓ2\ell_{2} normalized activations from the last hidden layer of VGGNet [48].
    norm I: 这些是来自 VGGNet [48] 最后一个隐藏层的 ℓ2subscript2\ell_{2} 归一化激活。

Question Channel: This channel provides an embedding for the question. We experiment with three embeddings –
问题通道：该通道为问题提供嵌入。我们尝试了三种嵌入——

  1. 1.

    Bag-of-Words Question (BoW Q): The top 1,000 words in the questions are used to create a bag-of-words representation. Since there is a strong correlation between the words that start a question and the answer (see Fig. 5), we find the top 10 first, second, and third words of the questions and create a 30 dimensional bag-of-words representation. These features are concatenated to get a 1,030-dim embedding for the question.
    词袋问题（BoW Q）：使用问题中出现频率最高的 1,000 个词来创建词袋表示。由于问题的开头词与答案之间存在强相关性（见图 5），我们找到问题中最常见的 10 个开头、第二和第三个词，并创建一个 30 维的词袋表示。这些特征被连接起来，得到问题的 1,030 维嵌入。

  2. 2.

    LSTM Q: An LSTM with one hidden layer is used to obtain 1024-dim embedding for the question. The embedding obtained from the LSTM is a concatenation of last cell state and last hidden state representations (each being 512-dim) from the hidden layer of the LSTM. Each question word is encoded with 300-dim embedding by a fully-connected layer + tanh non-linearity which is then fed to the LSTM. The input vocabulary to the embedding layer consists of all the question words seen in the training dataset.
    LSTM Q：使用一个隐藏层的 LSTM 来获取问题的 1024 维嵌入。从 LSTM 获得的嵌入是 LSTM 隐藏层中最后一个细胞状态和最后一个隐藏状态表示（每个为 512 维）的连接。每个问题词通过全连接层+tanh 非线性编码为 300 维嵌入，然后输入到 LSTM 中。嵌入层的输入词汇表由训练数据集中所有出现的问题词组成。

  3. 3.

    deeper LSTM Q: An LSTM with two hidden layers is used to obtain 2048-dim embedding for the question. The embedding obtained from the LSTM is a concatenation of last cell state and last hidden state representations (each being 512-dim) from each of the two hidden layers of the LSTM. Hence 2 (hidden layers) x 2 (cell state and hidden state) x 512 (dimensionality of each of the cell states, as well as hidden states) in Fig. 8. This is followed by a fully-connected layer + tanh non-linearity to transform 2048-dim embedding to 1024-dim. The question words are encoded in the same way as in LSTM Q.
    更深的 LSTM Q：使用具有两个隐藏层的 LSTM 来获取问题的 2048 维嵌入。从 LSTM 获得的嵌入是两个隐藏层中最后一个细胞状态和最后一个隐藏状态表示（每个为 512 维）的串联。因此，在图 8 中为 2（隐藏层）x 2（细胞状态和隐藏状态）x 512（每个细胞状态和隐藏状态的维度）。随后通过一个全连接层+tanh 非线性将 2048 维嵌入转换为 1024 维。问题词的编码方式与 LSTM Q 中的相同。

Multi-Layer Perceptron (MLP): The image and question embeddings are combined to obtain a single embedding.
多层感知器（MLP）：将图像和问题嵌入结合起来以获得单个嵌入。

  1. 1.

    For BoW Q + I method, we simply concatenate the BoW Q and I embeddings.
    对于 BoW Q + I 方法，我们简单地将 BoW Q 和 I 嵌入串联起来。

  2. 2.

    For LSTM Q + I, and deeper LSTM Q + norm I (Fig. 8) methods, the image embedding is first transformed to 1024-dim by a fully-connected layer + tanh non-linearity to match the LSTM embedding of the question. The transformed image and LSTM embeddings (being in a common space) are then fused via element-wise multiplication.
    对于 LSTM Q + I 和更深的 LSTM Q + norm I（图 8）方法，首先通过一个全连接层+tanh 非线性将图像嵌入转换为 1024 维，以匹配问题的 LSTM 嵌入。然后通过逐元素乘法将转换后的图像和 LSTM 嵌入（处于共同空间中）融合。

This combined image + question embedding is then passed to an MLP – a fully connected neural network classifier with 2 hidden layers and 1000 hidden units (dropout 0.5) in each layer with tanh non-linearity, followed by a softmax layer to obtain a distribution over K𝐾K answers. We also experimented with providing captions as input to our model. Similar to Table I, we assume that a human-generated caption is given as input. We use a bag-of-words representation containing the 1,000 most popular words in the captions as the caption embedding (Caption). For BoW Question + Caption (BoW Q + C) method, we simply concatenate the BoW Q and C embeddings.
我们还尝试将字幕作为输入提供给我们的模型。与表 I 类似，我们假设输入的是人工生成的字幕。我们使用包含字幕中最常见的 1000 个单词的词袋表示作为字幕嵌入（Caption）。对于 BoW 问题+字幕（BoW Q + C）方法，我们简单地将 BoW Q 和 C 嵌入连接起来。

7 Conclusion and Discussion
7 结论与讨论

In conclusion, we introduce the task of Visual Question Answering (VQA). Given an image and an open-ended, natural language question about the image, the task is to provide an accurate natural language answer. We provide a dataset containing over 250K images, 760K questions, and around 10M answers. We demonstrate the wide variety of questions and answers in our dataset, as well as the diverse set of AI capabilities in computer vision, natural language processing, and commonsense reasoning required to answer these questions accurately.
总之，我们介绍了视觉问答（VQA）任务。给定一张图像和一个关于该图像的开放式自然语言问题，任务是提供一个准确的自然语言答案。我们提供了一个包含超过 25 万张图像、76 万个问题和约 1000 万个答案的数据集。我们展示了数据集中广泛的问题和答案类型，以及准确回答这些问题所需的计算机视觉、自然语言处理和常识推理等多样化的人工智能能力。

The questions we solicited from our human subjects were open-ended and not task-specific. For some application domains, it would be useful to collect task-specific questions. For instance, questions may be gathered from subjects who are visually impaired [3], or the questions could focused on one specific domain (say sports). Bigham et al. [3] created an application that allows the visually impaired to capture images and ask open-ended questions that are answered by human subjects. Interestingly, these questions can rarely be answered using generic captions. Training on task-specific datasets may help enable practical VQA applications.
我们从人类受试者那里征集的问题都是开放式的，并不针对特定任务。对于某些应用领域，收集特定任务的问题会很有用。例如，可以从视觉障碍的受试者那里收集问题[3]，或者问题可以集中在某个特定领域（比如体育）。Bigham 等人[3]创建了一个应用程序，允许视觉障碍者拍摄图像并提出开放式问题，这些问题由人类受试者回答。有趣的是，这些问题很少能通过通用描述来回答。在特定任务的数据集上进行训练可能有助于实现实用的视觉问答应用。

We believe VQA has the distinctive advantage of pushing the frontiers on “AI-complete” problems, while being amenable to automatic evaluation. Given the recent progress in the community, we believe the time is ripe to take on such an endeavor.
我们相信视觉问答在推动‘AI 完全’问题前沿方面具有独特优势，同时适合自动评估。鉴于社区最近的进展，我们认为现在是进行此类努力的成熟时机。

Do questions and answers provide further information about the visual world beyond that captured by captions? One method for determining whether the information captured by questions & answers is different from the information captured by captions is to measure some of the differences in the word distributions from the two datasets. We cast this comparison in terms of nouns, verbs, and adjectives by extracting all words from the caption data (MS COCO captions for real images and captions collected by us for abstract scenes) using the Stanford part-of-speech (POS)666Noun tags begin with NN, verb tags begin with VB, adjective tags begin with JJ, and prepositions are tagged as IN. tagger [49]. We normalize the word frequencies from captions, questions, and answers per image, and compare captions vs. questions and answers combined. Using a Kolmogorov-Smirnov test to determine whether the underlying distributions of the two datasets differ, we find a significant difference for all three parts of speech (p << .001) for both real images and abstract scenes. This helps motivate the VQA task as a way to learn information about visual scenes; although both captions and questions & answers provide information about the visual world, they do it from different perspectives, with different underlying biases [20], and can function as complementary to one another.

Appendix V: “Age” and “Commonsense” of our model
附录 V：我们模型的“年龄”和“常识”

We estimate the age and degree of commonsense of our best model (deeper LSTM Q + norm I), selected using VQA test-dev accuracies). To estimate the age, we compute a weighted average of the average age per question, weighted by the accuracy of the model’s predicted answer for that question, on the subset of questions in the VQA validation set for which we have age annotations (how old a human needs to be to answer the question correctly). To estimate the degree of commonsense, we compute a weighted average of the average degree of commonsense per question, weighted by the accuracy of the model’s predicted answer for that question, on the subset of questions in the VQA validation set for which we have commonsense annotations (whether the question requires commonsense to answer it).
